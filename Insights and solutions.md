#### Insights 



1. South Africa, Nigeria, and Côte D’Ivoire are the largest shipment destinations, indicating these regions are major operational hubs in the supply chain network.
2. Air transportation dominates shipment operations, showing the organization prioritizes faster delivery for healthcare products.
3. Most shipments were delivered on time, indicating relatively stable logistics performance. However, a noticeable number of delayed shipments still exist and may affect operational reliability.
4. The supply chain system generally performs efficiently, with shipments arriving earlier than expected on average.
5. Certain countries experience significantly higher delays, suggesting possible transportation, customs, or logistics challenges in those regions.
6. A large portion of shipment operations depends heavily on a few vendors, especially SCMS from RDC.
7. Certain vendors contribute to higher average delivery delays, indicating inconsistent shipment performance.
8. Freight costs vary significantly across shipments, indicating operational complexity and cost variability in logistics operations.
9. Air Charter shipments are the most expensive transportation method due to premium logistics and urgent delivery requirements.
10. Shipment weight has only a weak positive relationship with freight cost, suggesting that additional operational factors influence transportation pricing.
11. ARV products dominate shipment operations, showing that the supply chain is heavily focused on pharmaceutical and HIV-related healthcare products.
12. A limited number of healthcare products contribute significantly to total shipment value and operational spending.





#### Recommended Solutions



| Problem                                    | One-Line Solution                                                                              |

| ------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| High shipment delays in certain countries  | Optimize regional logistics routes and improve customs coordination in delay-prone countries.  |

| Vendor-related delivery delays             | Implement vendor performance monitoring and SLA-based delivery tracking.                       |

| High freight costs                         | Consolidate shipments and optimize transportation mode selection to reduce logistics expenses. |

| AIR CHARTER shipments are expensive        | Restrict AIR CHARTER usage to emergency and high-priority deliveries only.                     |

| Overdependence on AIR transportation       | Adopt multi-modal transportation strategies using TRUCK and OCEAN where possible.              |

| High-risk high-value product shipments     | Prioritize tracking and secure transportation for high-value medical products.                 |

| Inconsistent shipment performance          | Develop a centralized supply chain monitoring dashboard for real-time operational visibility.  |
